Multiple Linear Regression in Python

This is an example of multiple linear regression on data points collected from a Combined Cycle Power Plant. A combined cycle power plant (CCPP) is composed of gas turbines (GT), steam turbines (ST) and heat recovery steam generators. In this use case we predict the hourly electrical energy output.
